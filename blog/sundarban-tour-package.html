Sundarban, the world's largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers a unique blend of adventure and tranquillity. Known for its rich biodiversity, including the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger, Sundarban is a paradise for nature enthusiasts and wildlife photographers. Our meticulously crafted Sundarban tour packages provide you with an opportunity to explore this enigmatic region, whether you're a seasoned traveller or a first-time visitor.

Best Time to Visit Sundarban

More than 5 year experience in this profession

The best time to visit Sundarban is between November and March. During these months, the weather is pleasant, and the chances of spotting wildlife, including the Royal Bengal Tiger, are higher. The monsoon season (June to September) brings heavy rainfall, making the area less accessible, but it also transforms the landscape into a lush, green paradise.

Explore the dense mangrove forests on guided safaris. With a bit of luck, you might spot the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger, along with spotted deer, wild boars, and crocodiles.
