If you're looking to experience the mystique of Sundarban's rich biodiversity, stunning landscapes, and its famed Royal Bengal Tigers, then our Sundarban Tour Package for 1 Night and 2 Days is the perfect getaway. Immerse yourself in nature, enjoy the cultural essence of the local communities, and get a close look at the beautiful wildlife of Sundarban. The tour package, priced between ₹4000 to ₹4500 per head (AC Room), offers a complete Sundarban experience without burning a hole in your pocket.

Detailed Daywise Itinerary

Day 1: Start Your Adventure into the Wild - Exploring the Sundarbans

Morning: Departure from Kolkata

Your journey begins bright and early. We offer two convenient pickup points in Kolkata:

  • First Pick-Up Point: Indian Museum Main Gate (Near Park Street Metro). The reporting time is 8:00 AM, and the departure time is 8:30 AM.
  • Second Pick-Up Point: P.C. Chandra Garden Gate No.1, Science City. The reporting time here is 8:30 AM, and we depart by 9:00 AM.

The journey to Godhkhali takes approximately 3.5 hours via an air-conditioned vehicle. Along the way, we’ll make a stop for breakfast and tea, so you can refresh and recharge for the adventure ahead.

Afternoon: Arrival at Sundarban Resort

1:30 PM: Upon reaching the Sundarbans, we’ll check into your accommodation. Our resort is located in a serene spot, just opposite the Sundarban Tiger Reserve. Surrounded by picturesque villages, dense forests, and the calming breeze of the nearby river, the atmosphere is perfect for a peaceful weekend getaway. You’ll have time to relax and settle in before heading out to explore.

2:00 PM: After getting settled, enjoy a sumptuous lunch prepared with local delicacies. The culinary experience alone will introduce you to the authentic flavors of Bengal.

Evening: Bird Watching & Sunset Views

4:00 PM: As the evening sets in, it’s time to head out on a tranquil boat ride to explore the Sundarban Bird Jungle. Witness the incredible variety of bird species that make this unique habitat their home. The Sundarbans is a paradise for bird watchers, offering sightings of rare and migratory birds against the backdrop of one of the most breathtaking sunsets you will ever see.

6:00 PM: After the sunset tour, we’ll return to the resort where you can freshen up and relax before the evening activities.

Night: Local Culture and Entertainment

7:00 PM: To give you a taste of local culture, we’ve arranged for a traditional local folk dance performance, accompanied by complimentary tea or coffee and snacks. The vibrant dances offer a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the Sundarban region.

8:00 PM: If you're visiting during the winter season, gather around a cozy bonfire, enjoying music and the cool, crisp air. Share stories, sing along, or simply bask in the warmth of the fire under a starry sky.

10:00 PM: Dinner will be served, featuring a delicious array of Bengali dishes. Afterward, retire for the night at the resort, enjoying the tranquility of nature.

Day 2: Jungle Safari and Exploration of Sundarban's Wildlife

Morning: Start of Sundarban Delta Safari

8:00 AM: After a refreshing night’s sleep, it’s time for the highlight of your Sundarban tour: the Sundarban Delta Safari. We’ll begin the day with an early morning boat ride, gliding through the labyrinth of rivers, creeks, and islands that form the Sundarban delta, the largest mangrove forest in the world.

8:30 AM: Our first stop will be the Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve, where our knowledgeable government-appointed tourist guide will join us for a guided jungle safari. The Sajnekhali Watch Tower is one of the best spots for catching a glimpse of the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger. While tiger sightings are rare, the sheer excitement of traversing through tiger territory adds an adrenaline rush to the tour.

Mid-Morning: Thrilling Boat Safari

9:30 AM: The boat safari continues through various rivers like Pirkhali, Sarakkhali, Gazikhali, and Deul Varani, where you’ll encounter a variety of wildlife. Keep your eyes peeled for spotted deer, wild boars, water monitor lizards, snakes, and the ever-watchful crocodiles basking on the riverbanks.

Additionally, birdwatchers can spot species such as kingfishers, herons, egrets, and if you're fortunate, you may even catch a fleeting glimpse of the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger swimming across the creeks!

Afternoon: Wildlife from a Unique Vantage Point

12:30 PM: One of the most thrilling parts of the safari is the visit to the Do-Banki Tiger Reserve Area. The highlight here is the 896-meter-long canopy walk. This raised, netted walkway allows you to walk through the forest canopy and observe wildlife from above, offering a unique perspective on the Sundarban jungle.

1:30 PM: Our next stop will be Panchamukhni, the famous 5-River Junction, where five different rivers meet. It’s a serene and scenic spot to reflect on your jungle adventures.

Evening: Return to Kolkata

4:00 – 5:00 PM: After completing the Sundarban tour, it’s time to head back to Kolkata. We’ll begin the return journey, making sure you arrive safely by 8:00 – 9:00 PM.
