Explore Sundarban: Standard 2-Night, 3-Day Adventure for Just ₹3999/- Per Person

Are you looking for an exciting yet budget-friendly escape into the wilds of Sundarban? Our Standard 2-Night, 3-Day Sundarban Tour Package is designed for travelers seeking an adventurous and memorable experience without stretching their budget. Priced at just ₹3999 per head, this package promises an economical way to explore the enchanting Sundarban National Park, renowned for its lush mangrove forests, diverse wildlife, and serene waterways.

Overview of the Standard Property Package

This 3-day, 2-night tour offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. It's an ideal choice for those who want to experience the wonders of Sundarban without breaking the bank. Our package includes comfortable lodgings, delicious meals, and thrilling activities, all while keeping the costs low.

Package Inclusions:

  • Transportation: Pick-up and drop-off service from Canning Railway Station.
  • Accommodation: Standard lodgings with options for AC and Non-AC rooms.
  • Meals: All major meals including breakfast, lunch, tea, snacks, and dinner.
  • Activities: Guided boat rides, jungle safaris, and cultural programs.
  • Permits: Jungle entry fees, boat permits, and still camera permissions.

Package Exclusions:

  • Hard or aerated drinks
  • Video camera charges
  • Miscellaneous expenses
  • Coolie charges
  • Permit fees for non-Indian guests

What to Bring:

  • Valid photo ID proof
  • Medicine for personal needs
  • Camera and binoculars
  • Light and comfortable baggage
  • Sun protection items (sunglasses, hat, sunscreen)
  • Cash (ATM access is limited)

Child Policy:

  • Age 1 to 4 years – Free
  • Age 4 to 8 years – 50% charge
  • Above 8 years – Full charge

Important Notes:

  • Itinerary is subject to change based on weather and tide conditions.
  • Food menu may vary based on availability.
  • Vehicle or boat changes may occur for safety reasons.
  • COVID-19 precautions include thermal screening, masks, hand sanitizers, and daily sanitization of boats and hotels.

Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

  • 10:00 AM: Arrival at Canning Railway Station. Our friendly staff will pick you up via Auto or Tata Magic and transport you to Godhkhali Launch Ghat, where you will board the boat. Please note that breakfast is not included on the first day.
  • 1:30 PM: Enjoy a delicious lunch prepared with local flavors.
  • 2:00 PM: Arrive at Pakhirala, a tranquil location opposite the Sundarban Tiger Reserve. Surrounded by a blend of village life, dense forests, and the gentle river breeze, Pakhirala offers a peaceful retreat and a perfect start to your Sundarban adventure.
  • 4:00 PM: Embark on a boat ride to explore the Sundarban Bird Jungle. The scenic beauty is enhanced by a stunning sunset view, making this boat ride a truly memorable experience. We will return to Pakhirala by 6:00 PM.
  • 7:00 PM: Experience a local folk dance performance accompanied by complimentary snacks and tea/coffee.
  • 10:00 PM: Enjoy a hearty dinner and settle in for a restful night at our hotel.

Day 2: Jungle Safari and River Exploration

  • 8:00 AM: Begin the day with a hearty breakfast before setting out for a thrilling boat safari across the Sundarban delta.
  • 8:30 AM: Guided by an experienced government tourist guide, embark on a jungle safari at the Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve. This area is renowned for its rich biodiversity and is a great spot to observe various wildlife species.
  • 9:30 AM: Navigate through the intricate network of rivers, islands, and small creeks. Our boat safari covers notable spots such as Pirkhali, Banbibi Varani, Sundarkhali, Khonakhali, and others. Keep your eyes peeled for spotted deer, crocodiles, wild boars, snakes, water monitor lizards, numerous bird species, and possibly the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger.
  • 12:30 PM: Visit the Do-Banki Tiger Reserve and its 896-meter canopy walk. This elevated walkway offers an exciting vantage point for wildlife viewing from a netted enclosure.
  • 1:30 PM: Continue your exploration with a visit to Panchamukhni, the 5-river junction, and Jharkhali Tiger Reserve Area, including another boat ride to its watchtower.
  • 5:00 PM: After a day filled with exhilarating activities, return to Pakhirala for relaxation.
  • 10:00 PM: Dinner will be served at the hotel.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration and Departure

  • 9:00 AM: After breakfast, take a leisurely walk through a local village, experiencing the authentic rural life of Sundarban.
  • 10:30 AM: Visit the historic Hamilton Bungalow and Rabindranath Tagore Bungalow. These landmarks provide a glimpse into the region's colonial past and its cultural heritage.
  • 12:00 PM: Transfer by boat back to the vehicle for your journey back to Kolkata. Lunch will be provided en route.
  • 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Arrive in Canning or Kolkata, concluding your Sundarban adventure.
