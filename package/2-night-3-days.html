Sundarban 3 Days 2 Nights Adventure Package

The Sundarban, a UNESCO World Heritage site and the largest mangrove forest in the world, is home to the famous Royal Bengal Tiger and a variety of wildlife. With our 3 Days and 2 Nights Sundarban Tour Package (₹5500/- to ₹6500/- Per Head), you can experience the essence of the wild, the culture, and the serenity of this unique ecosystem. This package is perfect for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking an escape from the daily grind.

Daywise Itinerary: 3 Days, 2 Nights Sundarban Tour

Day 1: Welcome to the Sundarbans

  • 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM: Pick-up from Indian Museum or Science City.
  • 2:30 PM: Arrival at the hotel near Sundarban Tiger Reserve. Check-in and relax.
  • 4:00 PM: Short boat ride to Sundarban Bird Jungle.
  • 6:00 PM: Return to the hotel for a cultural evening with snacks.
  • 10:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Wildlife Safari & Jungle Exploration

  • 8:00 AM: Breakfast and start Sundarban Delta Safari by boat.
  • 8:30 AM: Stop at the Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve.
  • 9:30 AM: Explore Pirkhali, Banbibi Varani, and Sundarkhali. Possible sightings include the Royal Bengal Tiger, spotted deer, crocodiles, and bird species.
  • 12:30 PM: Visit Do-Banki Tiger Reserve for a canopy walk.
  • 1:30 PM: Visit the Panchamukhni 5 River Junction.
  • 5:00 PM: Return to Pakhiralay. Evening at leisure.
  • 10:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Local Culture & Farewell

  • 9:30 AM: Local village walk and visit Hamilton & Rabindranath Tagore Bungalows.
  • 1:00 PM: Lunch en route and return to Kolkata.
  • 4:00 PM – 5:00 PM: Arrival in Kolkata. Tour ends.
