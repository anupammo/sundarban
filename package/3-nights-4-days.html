Sundarban Tour Package: 3 Nights 4 Days

Explore the Mystical Mangroves of Sundarban

Day 1: Arrival and Sundarban Exploration

  • Morning: Departure from Kolkata at 8:00 AM. Enjoy a scenic drive to Godkhali Jetty.
  • Afternoon: Board the boat and cruise through the Gomor and Durgadhwani rivers. Check-in at Sundarban Tiger Roar Resort. Enjoy a hot lunch.
  • Evening: Visit Bird’s Island and Sunset Point. Return to the resort for evening tea and snacks. Enjoy an Adivasi Jhumur dance performance. Dinner at 9:00 PM.

Day 2: Wildlife Safari and Village Tour

  • Morning: Breakfast at 7:00 AM. Cruise towards Sajnekhali Watchtower and Mangrove Interpretation Centre.
  • Afternoon: Deep Jungle Safari through creeks and dense forests. Spot wildlife like deer, water monitor lizards, monkeys, wild boars, crocodiles, and the Royal Bengal Tiger. Lunch on the boat or at the resort.
  • Evening: Walk through the native village of Jamespur. Learn about local crafts, cuisines, and lifestyle. Enjoy Baul Folk songs and dinner at 9:00 PM.

Day 3: Netidhopani Watchtower and Cultural Experience

  • Morning: Wake up to bird watching and a warm cup of tea. Breakfast at the resort.
  • Afternoon: Visit Netidhopani Watchtower and the 300-year-old Bon Bibi temple. Lunch onboard as we cruise back to the resort.
  • Evening: Free time for individual activities.

Day 4: Departure

  • Morning: Breakfast at 8:30 AM. Checkout and proceed to Gosaba. Visit Hamilton Bungalow and Rabindranath Tagore Bungalow.
  • Afternoon: Early lunch (packed) and transfer back to Kolkata.

  • All sightseeing
