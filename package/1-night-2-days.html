Sundarban Tour 1 Night 2 Days

Price: ₹4000 - ₹4500 Per Head

Detailed Daywise Itinerary

Day 1: Exploring the Sundarbans

Morning: Departure from Kolkata with two convenient pickup points:

  • 8:00 AM: Indian Museum Main Gate near Park Street Metro. Departure at 8:30 AM.
  • 8:30 AM: P.C. Chandra Garden Gate, Science City. Departure at 9:00 AM.
  • Enjoy a 3.5-hour drive to Godhkhali, with a tea and breakfast break on the way.

1:30 PM: Check-in at the Sundarban resort, located near the Tiger Reserve, surrounded by villages and rivers.

2:00 PM: Enjoy a delightful local lunch.

4:00 PM: Take a boat ride to explore the Sundarban Bird Jungle and witness a mesmerizing sunset.

7:00 PM: Enjoy a local folk dance performance with complimentary snacks.

8:00 PM: Winter bonfire and music session (winter only).

10:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Jungle Safari & Return

8:00 AM: Begin the Sundarban Delta Safari by boat, exploring rivers, creeks, and the Tiger Reserve.

8:30 AM: Guided jungle safari at Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve.

9:30 AM: Continue boat safari through various rivers and creeks to spot wildlife such as spotted deer, crocodiles, and, if lucky, the Royal Bengal Tiger.

12:30 PM: Visit Do-Banki Tiger Reserve with an 896-meter canopy walk.

1:30 PM: Stop at Panchamukhni, the famous five-river junction.

4:00 PM: Depart Sundarbans and return to Kolkata by 8:00 PM.

  • Camera or binoculars for wildlife viewing.
  • Light baggage and comfortable footwear.
