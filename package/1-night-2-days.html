Detailed Daywise Itinerary

Day 1 : Exploring the Sundarbans

Morning : Departure from Kolkata with two convenient pickup points:

01:30 pm Check-in at the Sundarban resort, located near the Tiger Reserve, surrounded by villages and rivers.

02:00 pm Enjoy a delightful local lunch.

04:00 pm Take a boat ride to explore the Sundarban Bird Jungle and witness a mesmerizing sunset.

07:00 pm Enjoy a local folk dance performance with complimentary snacks.

08:00 pm Winter bonfire and music session (winter only).

10:00 pm Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2 : Jungle Safari & Return

08:00 AM:Begin the Sundarban Delta Safari by boat, exploring rivers, creeks, and the Tiger Reserve.

08:30 AM:Guided jungle safari at Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve.

09:30 AM:Continue boat safari through various rivers and creeks to spot wildlife such as spotted deer, crocodiles, and, if lucky, the Royal Bengal Tiger.

12:30 PM:Visit Do-Banki Tiger Reserve with an 896-meter canopy walk.

01:30 PM:Stop at Panchamukhni, the famous five-river junction.

04:00 PM:Depart Sundarbans and return to Kolkata by 8:00 PM.

Essential Items to Carry:

  • Specific medicines for personal needs (e.g., cough, cold, stomach upset)
  • Valid photo ID proof during travel (e.g., Driving Licence, AADHAR)

  • Camera and binoculars for wildlife and landscape viewing
  • Light, easy-to-carry baggage
  • Comfortable footwear
  • Sunglasses, hat, and sunscreen
  • Cash, as ATM access is limited (at Gosaba)
