Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the Sundarban, one of the world's largest mangrove forests and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Our 1-Day Sundarban Tour offers a perfect blend of adventure, wildlife spotting, and serene beauty—all packed into a single day. Priced at just ₹1500 per head, this tour promises an enriching experience with meticulous planning to ensure a memorable visit.

Detailed Day-Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Explore the Mystical Sundarban

5:00 AM:
Departure from Kolkata

Upon arrival at Godkhali, board our mechanized, well-maintained boat equipped with comfortable beds and European toilets. This boat will serve as your floating base as you explore the intricate network of creeks and delta regions that define the Sundarban. Set sail into the heart of the mangrove forest, where you might encounter diverse wildlife, including crocodiles, water monitors, spotted deer, and an array of bird species. With a bit of luck, you could even catch a glimpse of the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat. During the boat ride, we will visit three prominent watchtowers: Sajnekhali, Sudhanyakhali, and Dobanki, offering panoramic views of the forest and its wildlife.

  • Comfortable footwear
  • Sunglasses, hat, and sunscreen
  • Cash, as ATM access is limited (at Gosaba)
