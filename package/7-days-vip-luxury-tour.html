Daywise Itinerary

Day 1 : Arrival in Kolkata

Welcome drink and check-in at a luxury hotel. Evening city tour of Kolkata.

Day 2 : Journey to Sundarbans

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to Sundarbans in a luxury vehicle. Check-in at a 5-star eco-friendly resort. Welcome dinner and cultural performance.

Day 3-6 : Explore Sundarbans

Daily jungle safaris with private guide. Bird watching & photography sessions. Visit to local villages & interaction with locals. Relaxation and spa services at the resort.

Day 7 : Return to Kolkata

Breakfast at the resort. Transfer back to Kolkata. Farewell dinner and overnight stay at a luxury hotel.

Day 8 : Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
