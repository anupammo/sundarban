Sundarban VIP Special Package Tour - 7 Nights, 7 Days

Experience the Majestic Sundarbans in Luxury

Package Highlights

  • Duration: 7 Nights, 7 Days
  • Accommodation: 5-star eco-friendly resorts
  • Transportation: Luxury vehicle from Kolkata
  • Meals: All-inclusive gourmet dining
  • Activities: Jungle safari, bird watching, cultural performances, and more
  • Amenities: Private guide, spa services, and VIP lounge access

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata

Welcome drink and check-in at a luxury hotel. Evening city tour of Kolkata.

Day 2: Journey to Sundarbans

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to Sundarbans in a luxury vehicle. Check-in at a 5-star eco-friendly resort. Welcome dinner and cultural performance.

Day 3-6: Explore Sundarbans

Daily jungle safaris with private guide. Bird watching and photography sessions. Visit to local villages and interaction with locals. Relaxation and spa services at the resort.

Day 7: Return to Kolkata

Breakfast at the resort. Transfer back to Kolkata. Farewell dinner and overnight stay at a luxury hotel.

Day 8: Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
