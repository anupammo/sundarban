Introduction: WBTC Sundarban Package Tour in December

December is an ideal time to explore the Sundarbans, thanks to its pleasant weather and optimal conditions for wildlife viewing. The West Bengal Tourism Development Corporation (WBTC) offers exclusive WBTC Sundarban package tours in December, designed to provide an unforgettable experience amidst the natural beauty and unique biodiversity of this region. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about these December packages and why they are the best choice for your winter getaway.

Why Choose WBTC Sundarban Package Tours in December?

Choosing a WBTC Sundarban package tour in December offers a range of benefits:

  • Expert-Led Tours: WBTC tours are conducted by knowledgeable guides who provide valuable insights into the region’s wildlife, ecology, and conservation efforts.
  • Luxury Accommodations: Enjoy comfortable and luxurious lodgings that cater to your every need, with a focus on relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Well-Curated Itineraries: The tours include thoughtfully planned itineraries that ensure you experience the best of the Sundarbans, including wildlife safaris, boat rides, and cultural interactions.
  • Safety and Comfort: WBTC prioritizes your safety and comfort, offering reliable transportation, health and safety measures, and support throughout your journey.
  • Exclusive December Deals: Benefit from special discounts and offers available exclusively for December tours, making it a cost-effective option for travelers.

Sample Itinerary for WBTC Sundarban Package Tour in December

Here is a typical itinerary for a WBTC Sundarban package tour in December:

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed by a WBTC representative and transferred to your accommodation. After checking in, enjoy a welcome dinner where you'll receive an overview of the tour schedule and safety instructions.

Day 2: Wildlife Safari Adventure

Start your day with an exciting wildlife safari in the Sundarbans National Park. Spot the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger and other unique wildlife such as saltwater crocodiles, spotted deer, and various bird species. Your guide will share fascinating details about the ecosystem and wildlife.

Day 3: Boat Cruise and Local Culture

Enjoy a serene boat cruise through the mangrove forests of the Sundarbans. The afternoon includes a visit to local villages where you can experience the local culture, traditional crafts, and regional cuisine.

Day 4: Bird Watching and Nature Exploration

Spend the day bird watching and exploring the diverse flora of the Sundarbans. Join guided nature walks to learn about the unique plant species and their role in the ecosystem.

Day 5: Departure

After a leisurely breakfast, check out of your accommodation and transfer back to your departure point. Reflect on your memorable experiences as you head to your next destination or return home.

Tips for a Memorable WBTC Sundarban Package Tour in December

  • Pack Appropriately: December in Sundarban can be cool, so bring warm clothing and comfortable footwear for outdoor activities.
  • Stay Hydrated: Ensure you drink plenty of water, especially during active tours.
  • Respect Wildlife: Follow your guide’s instructions to ensure the safety of wildlife and avoid disturbing their natural habitat.
  • Capture the Moments: Bring a camera to capture the stunning landscapes and wildlife encounters. Ensure it’s well-protected from moisture and dust.
  • Be Flexible: Weather conditions can affect your itinerary, so be prepared for possible changes and embrace the adventure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the WBTC Sundarban package tour in December?

The package typically includes transportation, accommodation, meals, guided tours, wildlife safaris, boat cruises, and cultural experiences. Check the specific package details for a complete list of inclusions.

How do I book a WBTC Sundarban package tour?

You can book your tour through the WBTC website or authorized travel agents. Ensure to check availability and secure your booking well in advance, especially for December tours.

What should I bring for the tour?

Essential items include comfortable clothing, warm layers, a good pair of walking shoes, a camera, sunscreen, insect repellent, and personal medications. Be sure to follow any additional recommendations provided by your tour operator.
